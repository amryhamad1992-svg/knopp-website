Our Mission

We believe every professional deserves to grow their presence on LinkedIn - regardless of how busy they are or how much budget they have.

LinkedIn is one of the most powerful platforms for business growth, lead generation, and professional networking. But let's be honest: creating engaging content consistently is hard. It takes time, creativity, and effort that most professionals simply don't have.

That's why we built Knopp.

The Problem We're Solving

Small businesses, consultants, and independent professionals know they should be active on LinkedIn. They see others building audiences, generating leads, and growing their businesses. But between client work, meetings, and running their operations, LinkedIn content creation often falls to the bottom of the list.

Why Knopp?

We wanted to create the easiest, most hassle-free way to stay active on LinkedIn. No more staring at a blank screen wondering what to post. No more spending hours crafting the perfect comment. No more expensive content agencies or ghostwriters.

With Knopp, you can turn any article into a compelling post in seconds. Screenshot a post you want to engage with, and get thoughtful comments instantly. Set your tone and industry once, and every piece of content sounds authentically like you.
