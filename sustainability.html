Sustainable AI

AI That's Kind to the Planet

We love AI, but we believe in using it responsibly. That's why sustainability is at the heart of everything we build.

Why Sustainable AI Matters

Every AI request uses energy. Large language models like GPT-4 and Claude are incredibly powerful, but they also consume significantly more computational resources than smaller, more efficient models.

At Knopp, we believe you shouldn't have to choose between great content and environmental responsibility. That's why we've made it easy to see the environmental impact of your choices and select more sustainable options when they work for you.
