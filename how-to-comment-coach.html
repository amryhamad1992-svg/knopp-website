How It Works

1

Screenshot the Post

When you see a LinkedIn post you want to engage with, take a screenshot. You can also share an image directly to Knopp from your photo gallery or use the share menu.

2

Upload to Comment Coach

Open Comment Coach in Knopp and select your screenshot. The AI reads the post content and understands the context, including the author's tone and topic.

3

Choose Your Intent

Select how you want to engage: agree and add value, ask a thoughtful question, share a related experience, or offer a different perspective. Each generates a unique comment style.

4

Review & Post

Knopp generates a comment tailored to the post. Review it, make any personal tweaks, then copy and paste it into LinkedIn. Watch your engagement grow!

Pro Tip

Commenting consistently on posts in your industry is one of the fastest ways to grow your LinkedIn presence. Aim for 5-10 thoughtful comments per day.
