AI Post Generation

Create engaging LinkedIn posts from articles, ideas, or topics in seconds with AI assistance.

How It Works

1

Choose Your Source

Start by selecting how you want to create your post. You can paste an article URL, enter a topic or idea, or use trending topics from the app. Knopp works with any content source.

2

Set Your Preferences

Pick your tone (professional, witty, inspiring), category (leadership, tech, marketing), and format (short, story, listicle). These settings shape how your post will sound.

3

Generate & Choose

Tap generate and Knopp creates 3 unique variations of your post. Each version takes a different angle on your content, so you can pick the one that resonates most.

4

Edit & Post

Fine-tune your chosen post if needed, add hashtags, and copy it directly to LinkedIn. Your post is ready to engage your network and grow your presence.

Pro Tip

Set up your personalisation profile first - Knopp will learn your industry, role, and speaking style to make every post sound authentically you.
